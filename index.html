Industry Projects

Voman Packaging Redesign and Videography

HypeNest LLC, Shanghai

During my internship at HypeNest, a Shanghai-based OEM/ODM startup, I worked on Voman - an active crossover audio system integrated in an in-car display. After identifying that the existing product packaging was larger than necessary and contributing to excessive shipping costs, I redesigned the packaging to reduce the product’s volumetric weight by 41%.

See details →

Key skills: CAD • Graphic Design • Videography • Mandarin-English Translation

Legacy Wellsite Scoping Project

Wood PLC, Brisbane

As part of my internship at Wood PLC, I contributed to a project focused on developing new inspection scopes for legacy wellsites that had previously been inspected without a scope. This involved interpreting historical inspection data and mechanical drawings to create scopes that could be integrated with NEXUS IC mobile inspections.

Key skills: Mechanical Drawings • Draw.io • Nexus IC • NDT Reports
