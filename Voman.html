Voman Packaging Redesign and Videography

HypeNest LLC, Shanghai

Role: Technical Communications and Global Marketing Intern

Duration: June 2025 - July 2025

Key skills: CAD • Graphic Design • Videography • Mandarin-English Translation

During my internship at HypeNest, a Shanghai-based startup developing automotive technology, I worked on Voman - an active crossover car audio system integrated into an in-car display. This involved redesigning the product packaging, as well as creating social media platforms from scratch, including an instagram page that garnered 2.3k impressions within two weeks of creation through targeted digital outreach.

After identifying that the existing product packaging was larger than necessary and contributing to excessive shipping costs, I redesigned the packaging to reduce the product’s volumetric weight by 41%. To achieve this, I created a model of the product in Autodesk Inventor from a visual inspection of the product so that accurate dimensions can be determined.

In addition to optimising the packaging dimensions, I also designed the external packaging, extending my contribution from engineering into graphic design. Multiple designs were created to pitch to the team, based on a new marketing style that another intern and I developed to shift their marketing strategy towards the US automotive electronics market.

The CAD model proved to be of significant use among the team, as it was later used for multiple purposes such as creating product animations for marketing materials, and the cover of the user manual. By exhibiting my skillset in CAD modelling and graphic design, I was also given the task of developing 2D drawings for wiring instructions for the user manual.

Throughout this project, I utilised skills in:

  • CAD: developing detailed 3D product and packaging models in Autodesk Inventor for visualisations and product animations for marketing materials, as well as 2D drawings for the user manual
  • Graphic Design: designed packaging graphics and layouts using Canva
  • Videography: filmed and edited marketing content using CapCut
  • Communication: provided verbal and written Mandarin–English translation to communicate and justify design decisions to the team.
